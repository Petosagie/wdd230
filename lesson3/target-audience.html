Target Audience

The target audience for this website is everybody especially those people interested in doing businesses in the Benin city area and environ. Those who are already members of Benin City Chamber of Commerce will also use the site. Those also interested in learning about Benin City history and events are also welcome to use the site. The website will be accessed from desktop, laptop, tablets and mobile device.
