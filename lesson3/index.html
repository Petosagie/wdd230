Site Purpose

This site is meant to showcase the Chamber of Commerce for a city, Benin-City in Edo state,Nigeria. The site will allow users to find businesses in the area. The site will include historical information and information about city events. Businesses can sign up for the Chamber here, and the "Join" page will include different membership levels for businesses. There will also be contact information about the Chamber of commerce. The Site will also help to promote and protect the interests of its Benin City business community at large through public policy advocacy, creation and facilitation of trade, investment and general business opportunities, provision of business development services and observance of highest standard of business ethics.
